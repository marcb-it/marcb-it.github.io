Corporate Headquarters Relocation — Network & Infrastructure Lead

Mid-size company HQ buildout · ~150 users · single building

Served as on-site lead for the IT side of a corporate headquarters relocation. Took the new building from empty walls to a fully operational network and end-user environment, and decommissioned the previous site with a clean cutover. Coordinated ISP turn-up, structured cabling, switch and wireless deployment, IP camera and alarm systems, and full office and conference-room setup.

What I did:

  • Configured a Cisco SG500-52MP switch and connected all networked devices; tested existing patches and cables in the building, terminated new Cat6 runs where needed, and built and tested new patch panels.
  • Deployed and configured Ubiquiti UniFi AC Pro access points and stood up the UniFi controller on a server to centrally manage the wireless environment.
  • Coordinated ISP turn-up (modem / ONT) and Cisco Meraki deployment for the new site.
  • Installed and configured Reolink and Amcrest IP cameras, then stood up a server running Blue Iris to manage and record camera feeds.
  • Oversaw installation of a networked alarm and access control system managed via Keri Systems software on a dedicated server.
  • Set up individual offices and the conference room: mounted TVs, configured network printers, and validated end-user connectivity prior to go-live.
  • Decommissioned the previous site’s network and coordinated equipment moves, transitions, and vendor activity to ensure a smooth cutover with minimal business impact.
